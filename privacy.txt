Privacy Notice for oneill.sh:

NGINX Access logs are stored on the web server for ten days. They are
used to count the number of unique connections each week for
analytical purposes. The logs are destroyed after the ten day period
and are not shared with any third party. The number of unique IP
addresses that connected per week is kept indefinitely. The IP
addresses are not kept.

No other data is stored.
